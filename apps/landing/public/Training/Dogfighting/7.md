# 7. Drill: Orbit a fixed target

**[Video: Basic drills](https://youtu.be/BUsOaQDAjc8)** — decoupled, take off & landing, tracking.

**Focus:** Orbit a fixed structure in decoupled with your nose locked on it; re-acquire with roll then pitch. One drill, one habit.

**Why it matters:** This is the same motion you'll use in a rate fight — nose on target while you move. Build it on a station before you add a moving ship.

Build muscle memory before theory. Load into Arena Commander (or Free Flight) and pick a fixed structure — **Security Post Kareah** (or any station in your AC map) is a good practice object.

**Goal:** Fly around it with your nose locked on one spot. Decoupled. Smooth circle, minimal input.

**Drill 1 — Orbit**  
Nose on the target. Add strafe (left or right and a little up/down as needed) and a touch of throttle so you're moving in a circle around it. In decoupled you'll keep your momentum; small corrections keep the orbit. Don't yaw to track — roll so the target is above or below you, then pitch. That's faster and builds the habit you'll use in a real fight.

**Drill 2 — Roll then pitch re-acquire**  
Pick a spot on the structure (e.g. a dish or panel). Look away, then re-acquire: roll so the target is above or below you, then pitch onto it. No yaw. It should feel faster than yawing across. Repeat until it's automatic.

Try the orbit close in (tight) and a bit farther out. If you lose the nose, re-acquire with roll then pitch.

**Checkpoint:** You can orbit a fixed target in decoupled with your nose on it for at least one full circle, using roll and pitch to keep the nose on target.
