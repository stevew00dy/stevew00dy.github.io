# Structure overview

Every lesson has **one** learning objective. Text first; video or other formats can follow. Use this structure so lessons stay consistent and easy to break into clips later.

---

## Template (each lesson)

```markdown
# N. [Title — one thing]

**Focus:** [One sentence: what this lesson is the only place that teaches.]

**Why it matters:** [1–2 sentences. Why now, in this order.]

[Body: 2–5 short sections, all supporting the single focus. Use **bold** for terms. Bullets or short paragraphs.]

**Checkpoint:** [One thing the learner can do or say to show they got it. No extra objectives.]

**Next:** [Optional. One line: "Lesson N+1 covers…"]
```

- **Title** = the one thing (e.g. "6DOF", "Decoupled and the brake", "The merge").
- **Focus** = the single takeaway. If the lesson had two focuses, split it.
- **Checkpoint** = one measurable outcome. Not a list.
- **Next** = optional; helps navigation.
