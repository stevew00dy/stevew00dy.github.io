# 16. Multi-target

**Focus:** When more than two ships are in the fight, target priority and when to disengage matter more than raw aim.

**Why it matters:** Tunnelling one target while another gets behind you is a fast way to die. So is staying when you're outnumbered and separated.

**Target priority**  
Focus the ship that's already damaged or the one that's about to get a shot on your wingman. Don't tunnel one target while another gets behind you.

**2v1** — you're at a disadvantage. Use range and evasion; try to separate them so you fight one at a time. If you can't, create distance and run. Surviving is a win.

**2v2** — communicate if you can. Call targets, call when you're disengaging. Two pilots with nose authority on different targets can clear a fight fast.

Disengage when you're low on boost, low on hull, or outnumbered and separated (see lesson 15).

**Checkpoint:** You've been in a multi-target fight (AC or PU) and thought about target priority and when to leave.
