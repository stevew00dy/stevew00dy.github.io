# 8. Drill: Tracking

**[Video: Basic drills — decoupled, take off & landing, tracking](https://youtu.be/BUsOaQDAjc8)**

**Focus:** Keep your nose (or pip when you have a target) on a fixed point while you move. Prep for keeping the pip on a moving ship.

**Why it matters:** When the target is another ship, the pip tells you where to put the nose; the same tracking skill applies. Fixed point first, then live target.

Keep your crosshair (or pip, when you have a target) on a fixed point while you move.

**Setup:** Arena Commander or Free Flight. Pick a fixed point — at Kareah or any station, a structure, or part of the environment.

**Drill 1 — Pass-by:** Fly toward the point, then past it. Keep the nose on it as long as you can. Slow pass first; then try at different angles. Use roll then pitch to re-acquire if you lose it.

**Drill 2 — Circle and track:** Orbit around the point (like lesson 7) but focus on keeping the nose exactly on one spot for the whole orbit. Smooth, steady.

**Drill 3 — Moving past:** Fly in a straight line and pick a point off to the side. Roll so it's above or below you, then pitch to put the nose on it and hold it as you fly by. Repeat from the other side.

The idea is consistency: your hands learn to keep the nose on a fixed target while you're moving. When the target is another ship, the pip tells you where to put the nose; the same tracking skill applies.

**Checkpoint:** You can keep your nose on a fixed point while flying past it and while orbiting; you're using roll and pitch to stay on target.
