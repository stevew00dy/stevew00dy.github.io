# 17. Countermeasures

**Focus:** Flares and chaff break or confuse missile lock; missiles are pressure or finishers, not the main event. Guns win fights.

**Why it matters:** So you don't waste boost panicking on missiles, and so you don't rely on missiles when you should be working on pip discipline.

**Flares and chaff**  
They break missile lock or confuse seekers. Use them when you get a missile lock warning or when you see a launch — timing matters. Some missiles are flare-resistant, some chaff-resistant; the HUD usually indicates type. In Arena Commander you can test countermeasure bindings and get used to the lock tone and launch cues.

**Missiles**  
They can get kills, but skilled pilots often avoid them. Use missiles when it makes sense (e.g. on a disengaging target); don't depend on them. **Guns are the main event; missiles are pressure or finishers.**

**Checkpoint:** You know how to fire countermeasures and that guns, not missiles, are the core of PvP.
