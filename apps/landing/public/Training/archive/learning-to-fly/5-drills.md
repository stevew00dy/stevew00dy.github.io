# 5. Drills — nose on target, orbit, roll then pitch

**Description:** Structured practice at a station. These are the drills that build muscle memory.

**Image:** /lesson-thumbnails/flying-5.svg  
**Video:** https://youtu.be/vnkJymjOpf4

---

Load into Arena Commander (practice mode — no cost, instant respawn) and fly to a fixed reference like a station.

**Drill 1 — Nose on target:** Roll so the station is above you. Pitch (and strafe up if needed) to keep the nose on one spot as you fly past. Slow pass, repeat until it feels steady.

**Drill 2 — Orbit:** Nose on the station, add side strafe and a little throttle to fly in a circle around it. Decoupled holds the orbit smoothly with minimal input. Try it in coupled too — you'll feel the difference (you have to tap constantly).

**Drill 3 — Pole drill:** Orbit as close as you can to the structure without hitting it. Builds tight control.

**Drill 4 — Roll then pitch:** Pick a spot on the station (e.g. a dish). Look away, then re-acquire: roll so the target is above or below, then pitch onto it. No yaw. This should feel faster than yawing across.

**Checkpoint:** You can orbit a station with nose on it (decoupled) and re-acquire a point using roll then pitch.
