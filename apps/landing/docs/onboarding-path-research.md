# Getting Started — research-backed onboarding path

## What the game and community actually do

**First-time flow (RSI official):**
- Choose **Stanton** (not Pyro) for a safer start. Choose one of four cities: **New Babbage, Area18, Lorville, Orison** — that’s your first spawn.
- **Spawn rule:** “You will wake up at the **last landing zone you flew to that has hab facilities**.” So spawn follows where you last landed; it’s not fixed until you set it.
- **Setting spawn:** Go to a **medical facility** (hospital or clinic), use the **regeneration terminal** (Olympus Insurance / medbed), choose **Regeneration → Transfer Imprint**. That location becomes your respawn point. No cost, change anytime. **Orbital/Lagrange stations** = faster return to space (no tram); **cities** = more shops and facilities.
- **NPE (tutorial):** Optional when first loading in. Covers movement, interaction (F, PIT), navigating to spaceport, ASOP, first flight. Many still feel lost after it or skip it — so our path supports both.

**ASOP (ship):**
- **Retrieve** = ship is at this location, ready to use.
- **Claim** = ship was destroyed or is elsewhere; wait for it (or expedite for a fee).
- First time at a new spaceport you may need to **Deliver** then **Retrieve** (official doc).

**MobiGlas (F1):**
- **Comms** = request takeoff/landing (ATC). Essential so you don’t get fined or blocked.
- **Starmap (F2)** = set destination, quantum travel.
- **Contracts** = missions (e.g. Mercenary for bunkers, Delivery, etc.).

**First money-making (community 2024–25):**
- **Bunker missions** often recommended: Contracts → Mercenary, intro bunker, 15k+ aUEC, build rep. Need basic weapon/armour, any ship (fighter with interior or cargo helps).
- **Delivery** still exists but is more local (same city/area); good for “fly A→B” confidence.
- **Mining / trading** = next step once they want a profession. Hand-mining is low barrier.

**Pain points that make people quit:**
- Don’t know what happens when they die or where they’ll wake up.
- Can’t find ship or spaceport (orientation in city + tram).
- Don’t know how to set spawn and end up stuck somewhere they hate.
- Overwhelmed by “what do I do?” — need one clear first activity.

## Recommended path (order of lessons)

1. **Death, respawn, and setting your spawn**  
   What happens when you die. Where you wake (last hab location). How to *set* your spawn (regen terminal, Transfer Imprint) so you’re not stuck. Getting your ship back (ASOP: Retrieve vs Claim). One checklist so a bad run doesn’t make them quit.

2. **MobiGlas, spaceport, and your ship**  
   MobiGlas (F1): Comms for ATC, Starmap (F2), Contracts. How to get to the spaceport (tram, signs). ASOP: Retrieve/Claim, elevator to hangar. One simple route (e.g. city → orbital station and back) so they’re not lost.

3. **Your first flight (and first landing)**  
   Take off (request ATC), leave atmo, quantum, land or dock, request landing. One full loop they can repeat. Then one repeatable activity (bunker or delivery) so they feel progress.

4. **What to focus on first**  
   Don’t do everything. Pick one: bunkers (Mercenary tab, good starter), delivery, or mining. One loop, then branch. Tools that help (Loadout Planner, Armour Tracker, SPViewer, etc.) when they need them.

## Principles

- **Match the game:** Use correct terms (regen, Transfer Imprint, ASOP Retrieve/Claim, F1 Comms, Stanton vs Pyro).
- **Answer in order:** Death → Where’s my ship / how do I get around → How do I fly → What do I do for money.
- **One loop, then expand:** One route, one flight, one activity type. Then add next step.
- **Don’t duplicate NPE:** We assume they may do tutorial; we fill gaps (death, spawn, ATC, first mission type, tools) and reinforce.
