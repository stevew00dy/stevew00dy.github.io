# uNoob Content Plan

## Brand Position

**Sherpa Steve** — calm, clear, step-by-step Star Citizen guides for new and returning players. No hype, no noise. One ad at the start, then uninterrupted.

**Three content formats:**

1. **Guides** — structured, searchable, problem-solving ("How to...")
2. **Sherpa Sessions** — live guided runs with real new players, uncut
3. **Quick Tips** — short, shareable clips (under 5 mins)
