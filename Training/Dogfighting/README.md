# Dogfighting ? path overview

Ship-v-ship PvP from first principles. **Star Citizen 4.6+.** Assumes you can take off and fly a bit; we take you through foundations, drills, theory, then each stage of a fight. **17 lessons, one focus each.**

---

## Structure (locked in)

The path is six blocks. Order is intentional: basics ? where to practice ? drills ? theory ? stages in practice ? beyond 1v1.

| Block | Lessons | Purpose |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **Foundations** | 1?4 | Why dogfighting, HUD, basic controls (6DOF + inputs), settings. Everything you need before you fly. |
| **Where to practice** | 5 | Arena Commander ? where it is, why we use it, setup. |
| **Drills** | 6?8 | Decoupled + brake, orbit a fixed target, tracking. Muscle memory before theory. |
| **Theory** | 9?12 | Stages of a fight, key terms (glossary), neutralisation, turn rate and SPViewer. The words and concepts. |
| **Stages in practice** | 13?15 | Merge, turning duel and evasion, disengage. Apply the stages. |
| **Beyond 1v1** | 16?17 | Multi-target, countermeasures. |

**Consider adding (from ref 4 ? no need to copy the list):**
- **Trajectory cutting / cut the circle** ? Cut across to where they're going instead of following their orbit; get back on top when at disadvantage. (Glossary + duel.)
- **Don't rely on forward** ? Use strafe to get into position; a little forward to stay close; too much forward pushes you away. (Neutralisation 11.)
- **Push and pull** ? You can't always be aggressor or defender; sometimes neutralise and let them come, sometimes push in. (Glossary + duel.)
- **Too much circle strafe** ? Constantly circling pushes you away; they hold then push in when you're extended or out of ammo. (Duel 14, common mistake.)
- **Mid-range vs knife** ? Short comparison: mid-range approach easier to hit; knife approach tighter, fewer hits on you. (Duel 14 or key terms.)
