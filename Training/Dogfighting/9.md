# 9. Stages of a fight

**Focus:** Every fight has three stages — merge, duel, disengage — and knowing which you're in helps you decide what to do next.

**Why it matters:** Without this, a fight feels like chaos. With it, you can name what's happening and apply the right lesson (merge, turning duel, or disengage).

**1. Merge**  
Closing into effective weapon range so the fight can begin. The moment you're in range and committed. How you merge (delta, NAV vs SCM, approach angle) sets up the next stage — we cover that in lesson 13.

**2. The duel (rate fight)**  
Both ships orbiting, matching turn rates, trying to get and keep their nose on target. This is the turning contest; lesson 14 breaks down nose authority, pip neutralise, and evasion.

**3. Disengage**  
Breaking off: turn away, build distance, go to NAV, boost out. You do this when you're low on boost, low on hull, outnumbered, or need to reset. Lesson 15 covers when and how.

**Checkpoint:** You can name the three stages (merge, duel, disengage) and say which one you're in during a fight or replay.
